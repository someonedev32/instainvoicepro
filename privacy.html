2) Data We Collect

The App may collect or process the following types of information, depending on features you use:

A. Identifiers

  • App User ID (internal identifier used to manage subscription access and restore purchases).
  • Device identifiers (may be used for fraud prevention and reliable purchase validation).

B. Purchase Information

  • Purchase history and subscription status (e.g., active/expired) related to Apple In-App Purchases.

C. User Content (Optional / On-device)

  • Invoices, line items, notes, and other content you create in the App may be stored locally on your device. If you enable syncing features in the future, this policy will be updated accordingly.

Important: We do not collect sensitive categories (e.g., health, precise location, contacts) unless a feature explicitly requires it. If we add such a feature, we will update this policy.

3) How We Use Data

We use collected information for the following purposes:

  • App functionality: to enable core features and maintain your subscription access.
  • Purchase validation: to confirm purchases, handle refunds, and restore entitlements.
  • Customer support: to respond to support requests (when you contact us).
  • Security: to detect fraud or abuse and protect the App.

4) Sharing & Third Parties

We share data only as needed to operate the App and provide subscriptions. We do not sell your data.

Third-party services we use

  • Apple App Store / StoreKit — processes payments and subscription management.
  • RevenueCat — helps manage subscription status and entitlements across devices. RevenueCat may process identifiers and purchase information to provide this service.

These providers process data under their own terms and privacy policies. We recommend reviewing them: Apple and RevenueCat.

5) Subscriptions & Payments

Subscriptions are offered as auto-renewable subscriptions through Apple In-App Purchases. Billing, renewals, cancellations, and refunds are handled by Apple.

  • You can manage or cancel subscriptions in your Apple ID settings.
  • Free trials (if offered) convert to paid subscriptions unless cancelled before the trial ends.
  • We may use RevenueCat to verify subscription status and unlock premium features.

6) Data Retention

We retain data only as long as necessary to provide the App and comply with legal obligations. Purchase-related records may be retained for accounting, fraud prevention, and support.

7) Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards designed to protect information. However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

8) Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or restrict the use of your personal data. To request help, contact us at info@instaserver.net.

For subscription billing and refunds, you must use Apple’s subscription management tools.

9) Children’s Privacy

The App is not intended for children under 13 (or the minimum age required in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.
